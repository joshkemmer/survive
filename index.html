Analog synth mastery.

Finding location

Formed in 2009 in Austin, TX, the prolific experimental synth quartet S U R V I V E has released numerous albums, EPs, and singles. They’ve contributed to the soundtracks of the indie horror film ‘The Guest’ and the Netflix series ‘Stranger Things’.

The band is known for their fresh, bewildering update of vintage horror and sci-fi film scores as well as experimental ambient music and minimal wave. Their recorded output ranges from longform space explorations to hazy lo-fi electro. Their most recent album is RR7349.

S U R V I V E has appeared at numerous esteemed festivals across the US including Moogfest, Levitation Fest, Fun Fun Fun, Beserktown, Day in The Desert, etc. and have played alongside acts like Goblin, John Maus, Tame Impala, Lightning Bolt, and many others.
